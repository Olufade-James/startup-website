ABOUT US

Our Agency is an independent practice established in 1946 specialising in the valuation and sale of businesses and commercial premises and other related professional services. Whether buying or selling, renting or leasing, you can be assured of the personal attention of one of the Partners who are members of the Institution of Commercial & Business Agents and the National Association of Estate Agents.

Our offices are located in the centre of Oxford providing convenient access for our marketing area which includes the counties of Oxfordshire, Bath & North East Somerset, Berkshire, Buckinghamshire, Gloucestershire, Northamptonshire, Warwickshire, Worcestershire and Wiltshire.

Business Consulting has helped a variety of organizations grow revenue, improve profitability, implement new systems and facilitate the buying and selling of companies. With RMBC's assistance, companies have increased year-over-year revenue growth above market growth rates through the analysis and implementation of competitive pricing guidelines and the negotiation of customer agreements. Profitability improvement achieved through detailed reviews of company spending, evaluation of organizational structure, and implementation of new systems providing operational efficiencies. RMBC has represented companies on both the buying and selling sides of the negotiation.
